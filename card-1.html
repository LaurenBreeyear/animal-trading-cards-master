African Lion

African lions are the only cats that live in groups called pride's. In each pride the females out number the males.

  • Scietific Name: Panthera leo
  • Group Name: Pride
  • Weight: 265 to 420 pounds
  • Diet: Carnivore
    •

    African lions live in sub-Sahara Africa in group called prides. As the their offspring, cubs, grow up the felmale stay in the pride while the males drift off and create their own. Within the pride, the female lions are the primary hunters. Which contradicts the fierce and manly image connected to the image of the male lion.
